## 🎹 The Creators & Catalysts

### Dr. Robert "Bob" Moog

* **Video 1:** [Moog (Documentary)](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XRg8R-00mjs) (Published by Blame)
* *What to look for:* This is the definitive, feature-length documentary exploring Bob's life, his creative philosophy, and his deep interaction with the musicians who used his instruments.


* **Video 2:** [A Brief History of the Minimoog Part I](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sLx_x5Fuzp4) (Published by Moog Music)
* *What to look for:* A beautifully produced deep dive into how Bob and his team condensed massive, complex studio wall-modulars into the portable, retail-ready Model D.



### Herb Deutsch

* **Video 1:** [GIANTS | Herb Deutsch](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5hTyTHm1J_c) (Published by Moog Music)
* *What to look for:* An intimate, long-form retrospective interview filmed shortly before his passing, where Herb explains his 1963 collaborative workshops with Bob and how he pushed for a keyboard interface.


* **Video 2:** [The Mini Moog Synthesizer with Herb Deutsch](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=87l3rcttb5c) (Published by Michael Sterling)
* *What to look for:* Herb directly demonstrating and explaining the building blocks of the Minimoog, highlighting his perspective as both a composer and co-developer.



### Wendy Carlos

* **Video 1:** [1970: WENDY CARLOS and her MOOG SYNTHESISER | Music Now](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UsW2EDGbDqg) (Published by BBC Archive)
* *What to look for:* A rare archival broadcast showcasing Wendy in her studio context, painstakingly constructing the electronic orchestral tracks that put the Moog brand on the map.


* **Video 2:** [1969 short Moog Synthesizer demonstration by Wendy Carlos](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZrtTDzd-XS8) (Published by soup)
* *What to look for:* A brief historical look at Carlos interacting with her custom monophonic multi-cabinet modular system right around the height of the *Switched-On Bach* phenomenon.

## ⚡ The Performers & Virtuosos

### Keith Emerson

* **Video 1:** [Keith Emerson breaks down the moog](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NpgS9prNZT4) (Published by sfodallday)
* *What to look for:* Keith standing right in front of his iconic, massive "Monster Moog" modular rig, explaining how the patches work, the sample-and-hold modules, and his famous ribbon controller.


* **Video 2:** [Keith Emerson - Lucky Man Moog Solo](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MRYx0ySEhjk) (Published by Mybabybearsimba)
* *What to look for:* Vintage performance footage showing the explosive sonic impact of the portamento gliding pitch-wheel work that defined progressive rock synth solos.



### Rick Wakeman

* **Video 1:** [How Bob Moog Changed Everything | Rick Wakeman](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oIuJZhDDfBg) (Published by Rick Wakeman Fan Channel)
* *What to look for:* Rick detailing his personal relationship with Bob Moog and discussing how the instrument entirely shifted the landscape of live performance.


* **Video 2:** [Yes Interviews: Rick Wakeman with Bob Moog on his first Mini Moog](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWT63Opnk6A) (Published by Yes Source)
* *What to look for:* Archival audio/interview recounting Rick's first encounter with the instrument, buying it from an actor who thought it was broken because it only played one note at a time!



### Bernie Worrell

* **Video 1:** [Parliament Bernie Worrell - Flashlight - Moog Sub 37 Synth Sounds](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G2B7Rt_VA0M) (Published by Synth Sounds)
* *What to look for:* A breakdown reconstruction tracking how Bernie layered distinct Minimoog oscillators and dialed in the specific, snappy 24dB filter envelopes to build the ultimate funk bassline.


* **Video 2:** [Bernie Worrell at Moog in the am](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A4GEGkkA_wM) (Published by Howard Cohen)
* *What to look for:* Historical raw footage of Bernie visiting the Moog facility, showcasing his signature improvisational approach and fluid hand style on the keys.
